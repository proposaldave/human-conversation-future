Product surface

Four complete modules, one simple story.

Human Conversation does not put AI between community members. It gives trusted humans the operating system to know who needs attention, what was learned, and what should happen next.

Queue

Who needs a conversation?

Prioritized people and moments: disconnection signals, empty events, returning members, and possible hosts.

Signal

What changed after the conversation?

CallInsights turn human context into the useful signals: timing, comfort, social pull, and fit.

Recommendation

What should happen next?

Suggested invites, group matches, host introductions, schedule changes, and follow-up tasks.

Learning loop

Did it work?

Outcomes make the next recommendation sharper while the human relationship stays in front.

Trust and governance

AI supports the relationship. It does not replace it.

Trusted humans stay in front.

Owners, staff, hosts, and members create the relationship. AI prepares, listens, structures, and remembers.

Raw conversation is not the product.

Only approved, useful staff memory becomes durable context. Raw transcripts and sensitive notes stay guarded.

Every write has a gate.

Staff-only observations, follow-ups, registrations, and outbound messages require human approval.
